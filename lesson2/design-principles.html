Rule of Thirds

Corsair

corsair.com

The contents of the page is neatly seperated virtically by thirds. At almost any given time on the site there are about three elements that are visable and easily processed. The eye is naturally drawn to the individual sections.

White Space/Clean Design

Asus

asus.com

This site does a good job keeping elements grouped where needed for readability. The site doesn't appear too busy or overwhelmed with content and elements, and the elements that do take up space aren't too distracting themselves.

Repetition

Pokemon

pokemon.com

The cards on the site are divided evenly and are a pattern all the way down the page. The repetition of image, solid background, and text gives the whole site a uniform look along with keeping individual elements clear to the viewer.
