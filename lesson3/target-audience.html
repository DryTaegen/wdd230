Audience

Target Audience

The audience for this site is any entrepreneur who is looking to start a business or to expand their existing business into our neck of the woods. We offer information on events and services that can help anyone in business here promote their work and attract more people. Our site caters to any business person who is interested in having their desires lobbied here at a local level.

Personas

Person one: Snowbird retire

I am a 62-year-old retired buisnessman who loves skiing and is lookin for a place to settle down and open up a small shop geared towards helping people get their essentials heading up for a day in the mountain. I was interested in joining the chamber to better find opportunites to advertise my new shop.

Person two: Undecided developer

I am an established developer from Colorado and primarily focus on residential suberbs for development. I came to this site looking for the opportunities that various cities in the Salt Lake valley offered. I found Cottonwood Heights to be satisfactory and have plans on developing there soon.
